About Us

Welcome to TailTalesReunion, the leading pet lost management system in Chennai, TN. We understand the deep bond between pet owners and their furry friends, and our mission is to help reunite lost pets with their loving families.
With our advanced technology and extensive database, we provide a seamless platform for reporting lost pets, searching for found pets. and connecting pet owners with helpful resources Our dedicated team is committed to providing excellent service and support to both pet owners and those who have found lost pets. Together, let's bring tails back home!

Lucky The Dog

Lucky, a husky, disappeared during a camping trip in a remote area of the Rocky Mountains. His owners searched for weeks, fearing the worst. Miraculously, Lucky was found by a group of hikers who had seen the missing dog's picture on our website. Lucky had lost weight but was otherwise in good health and was joyfully reunited with his family.

Max,Loving Dog

Max, a playful Golden Retriever, slipped out of the yard one day and embarked on an adventure. After weeks of searching, his family received a call from a family in a nearby town who had found Max and saw missing post on our website and taken care of him. Max's family rushed to the new location and was greeted with enthusiastic tail wags and doggy kisses. Max's adventure had a happy ending.
